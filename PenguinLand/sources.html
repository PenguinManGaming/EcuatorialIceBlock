Where are the sources?

My source is that i made it the fuck up


Generally when writing Alternative History scenarios i don't list my sources because i don't keep record of from where i got my information from, or even take record of what i've readed as i get my information from videos, books, articles and most of the time i cannot really pin down from where i got the information. The other reason is that since this is not a professional historical work but a work of fiction, i don't really have the need to have the most precise information, as the objetive is not to educate.
